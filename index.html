How It Works

From log line to Slack alert
in under 60 seconds

OpsLens AI runs a continuous intelligence loop so your team always knows what broke, why it broke, and who should fix it.

1
Ingest Logs
Connect any log source — CloudWatch, Datadog, direct API, or our SDK. OpsLens ingests, normalizes, and indexes every log line in real time.
2
Detect & Deduplicate
AI-powered error detection groups related log lines into incidents, eliminating duplicate noise before it ever hits your team's Slack.
3
RAG Enrichment
Our retrieval engine pulls relevant Jira tickets, GitHub PRs, commit history, and Slack threads to build full context around each incident.
4
AI Diagnosis
Claude and GPT-4 analyze the enriched context to generate a root cause hypothesis, severity assessment, and suggested remediation steps.
5
Smart Routing
Rule-based routing sends the incident to the right Slack channel — payment team, infra team, or on-call — with zero false channels.
6
RRT Brief Auto-Generated
A Rapid Response Triage brief is created automatically, giving your engineers a structured starting point the moment they engage.
